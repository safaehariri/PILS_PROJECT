We're a team of 5 enthusiastic developers who created ReviewIT to make online shopping easier.

Instead of spending a lot of time reading disorganized reviews to choose a product, Review It provides a summarized analysis for you.

The circles depict the percentages of positive and negative reviews for the product.

After that, you'll find the positive and negative percentages for important product features, along with corresponding positive and negative comments.
